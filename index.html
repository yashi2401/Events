WIE IEEE ADGITM organized a webinar WIEInspire: To pursue studies abroad in collaboration with Hello Study Global. The official publicity partner of the event was IPU BUZZ The guest speaker for the event was Mr. Naveen Rai– the faculty & Mentor for GRE, SAT, LSAT, GMAT, and the Founder at Hello Study Global. The event began with welcoming all the attendees, our Branch counselor, and the guest speaker. This was followed by some inspiring words from our Branch Counselor, Parthish Sir. The last part of the event was a QnA session where all the doubts of the attendees were addressed. The webinar concluded with a vote of thanks towards all the attendees, Parthish Sir, and our guest speaker. A total of 190+ students registered for the event, and about 100+ were present during the webinar.

EVENT NAME

20/10/2021

Event Title

Event Sub-Title

On 22nd April, 2021, the 4th IEEE PES Day was celebrated to commemorate the changing of name of Power Engineering Society to Power and Energy Society to focus on helping the environment and the society. Activities conducted in the month of April are counted towards PES Day celebration. This year the theme of PES Day was “Clean Energy Revolution”. IEEE PES ADGITM has been celebrating PES Day since 2018. This year, we conducted two webinars to celebrate the goal of IEEE PES, which is “To be the leading provider of scientific and engineering information on electric power and energy for the betterment of society, and the preferred professional development source for our members”. The first event, webinar on “Emerging Power Systems” delivered by S.V. Kulkarni, was organized in collaboration with the department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, ADGITM. It was conducted on 6th April, 2021 and was considered as a “Pre-PES Day Event”. The second event was a webinar on “Sustainable electrification, power system resilience, and the energy quadrilemma” delivered by Dr. Pierluigi Mancarella. It was our “Main PES Day Event” and was conducted on 21st April, 2021.
